How it works

From download to your first pop-in in about two minutes.

1

Install it

Download, drag PopIn into your Applications folder, and open it. It's notarized by Apple, so it just opens.

2

Sign in with Google

One tap — no new account, no new password. Your email is your identity.

3

Authorize family

Add a trusted person's Google email. Only the people you authorize can ever pop in — and you can remove anyone in one click.

4

Pop in — or glance

One click opens a warm, full-screen video window. Or glance first for a silent peek, just to see all's well.

Setting up the always-on Mac

The Mac you'll be checking in on does best with a one-time setup so it's always ready — even after a restart. PopIn's built-in guide walks you through each step.

Allow Camera & Microphone

PopIn asks the first time you open it. One tap each, and pop-ins always connect.

Keep it awake & reachable

Turn on in Settings so the Mac stays online for a pop-in (keep it plugged in, lid open).

Open at login + auto-login

So if it ever restarts, it comes right back on its own — no one has to be there to log in. (FileVault off.)

New in the app: a Setup Guide that detects what's done and walks you through the rest. ✨

Yours, and only yours

Nobody can pop in unless you explicitly authorize their email — and you can revoke it instantly. Video is a direct, encrypted link between your Macs. PopIn is a care tool, made for families.
